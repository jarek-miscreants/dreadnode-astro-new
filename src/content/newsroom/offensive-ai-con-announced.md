Offensive AI Con (OAIC) is the world's first conference exclusively focused
on the use of AI in offensive security. The event brings together red
teamers, AI researchers, and security engineers pushing the frontier of
agent-driven offense.

Expect deep technical talks, hands-on workshops, and working sessions on the
open problems that matter — evaluation, tool use, safety, and operational
deployment.
