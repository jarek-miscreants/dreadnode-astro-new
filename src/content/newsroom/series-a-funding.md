Dreadnode has closed a $14M Series A to continue building AI systems that
advance the state of offensive security.

The capital will fund research into agent capabilities, evaluations, and the
underlying infrastructure required to run offensive AI at scale — and will
support hiring across engineering, research, and applied security roles.
